Why travelling makes you smarter

It's not ordinary for people to travel especially if they have a lot of things to do back home. It's always useful to have piece of mind and clear vision of what to do with our life while we're young and free. Time management is the key to a successful life and without it we would have been lost in time. Below i will describe the reasons why travelling is very beneficial for you and your mental health.

  • Travel helps your brain function better and boosts creativity
  • We rewire our brains and we have great state of mind
  • We show more enthousiasm about life and we tend to be more optimistic than the average joe
  • As you travel you're exposed to more new people, cultures, and lifestyles than you are living in your homeland all the time
